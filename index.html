START GAME

INSTRUCTIONS :



  • Press on START GAME to start the game
  • There are two types of cars in the game, yellow car and red car
  • Yellow is the user car and red are the other cars
  • Your Goal is to drive the yellow car continously and score points as much as possible
  • You can control the yellow car using arrow keys
  • For every 1000 points you score, speed will be increased
  • As soon as you hit the red car the game will end.
